# 📄 Workflow Explanation

The **Internship Management Platform** operates through two primary user flows: **Student** and **Company**, each interacting with the system to complete the internship process efficiently.

---

# 🔹 Student Workflow

The process begins when a student **signs up or logs into the platform**. After authentication, the student is required to **create a profile and upload their resume**, which is essential for applying to internships.

Once the profile is complete, the student can **browse available internship opportunities** based on their interests and skills. Each internship listing provides detailed information such as:

- Role description  
- Requirements  
- Internship duration  

After selecting a suitable internship, the student proceeds to **apply**, sending their profile and resume to the company.

The student can then **track the application status** through their dashboard, which updates dynamically as the company reviews the application.

The application status can be:

- **Pending**
- **Accepted**
- **Rejected**

This provides transparency and keeps the student informed throughout the process.

---

# 🔹 Company Workflow

Companies begin by **registering or logging into the platform**. Once logged in, they can **post internship opportunities**, specifying:

- Requirements  
- Roles  
- Expectations  

After posting, companies receive applications from students. They can **view and review candidate profiles and resumes** directly through the platform.

Based on the evaluation, companies can:

- **Accept candidates**
- **Reject candidates**

Once a decision is made, the system updates the application status, which becomes visible to the student.

---

# 🔹 System Interaction

The platform acts as a **central bridge between students and companies**, ensuring:

- Smooth communication  
- Real-time application tracking  
- Efficient recruitment workflow
